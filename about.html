Founder

Kateryna Bratchyna

A professional actress who discovered that the stage prepares you for life's biggest moments.

With years of experience in theatre and film, I realized that the techniques we use on stage — presence, emotional truth, voice control, body awareness — are exactly what professionals, entrepreneurs, and leaders need to succeed.

BRAT Studio was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves to express themselves with confidence and authenticity. Whether you're pitching to investors, leading a team, or simply wanting to be heard — these skills can be learned.

I work in English, Ukrainian, and Russian, serving clients across Toronto and beyond.

"I use my professional acting skills to help people become more confident, expressive, and authentic in every role they play — in life, business, and relationships."
